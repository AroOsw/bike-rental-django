## Booking & Pricing

## How far in advance should I book my bike?

We recommend booking at least 72 hours in advance, especially during peak season (November-February) to
ensure availability of your preferred model.

## What's included in the rental price?

All rentals include: helmet, high-security lock, comprehensive insurance
coverage, our support hotline, basic maintenance, and route recommendations. Additional services like
GPS devices or tour guides are available for extra fees.

## Safety & Insurance

## What does your insurance cover?

Our comprehensive insurance covers accidental damage to the bike and liability for third-party injuries.

## Do I need a driving license to rent a bike?

No, a standard driving license is not required. We only require a valid government-issued ID (e.g.,
passport or national ID card) for verification purposes.

## What if my bike breaks down during the rental?

Contact our 24/7 hotline immediately. We provide free roadside assistance within Chiang Mai city limits,
or we'll arrange a replacement bike delivery within 2 hours. For areas outside the city, we coordinate
with local partners for quick solutions.
